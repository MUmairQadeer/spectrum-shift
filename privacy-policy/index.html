Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 16, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the "Spectrum Shift" mobile application (the "App") developed by Umair Qadeer. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Spectrum Shift is a standalone game. It does not require an internet connection to play, does not require you to create an account, and does not track your personal identity, location, or contacts.

3. Local Storage

The App may save game data (such as your high scores, lives, or current level) directly on your device using local storage. This data remains on your personal device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties. If you uninstall the App, this local data will be deleted.

4. Third-Party Services

Currently, the App does not use any third-party analytics or advertising services that collect user data.

5. Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any personal information, our App is safe for children of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. If we add new features (such as online leaderboards or advertisements) that require data collection in the future, this policy will be updated to reflect those changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at the email address provided on our Google Play Store listing.
